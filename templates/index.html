Fake News Detection System

This is a student-built project developed to analyze online news articles using Natural Language Processing and credibility scoring. The system evaluates article content, publisher reliability, and linguistic patterns to estimate the likelihood of misinformation.

How the Scoring Works

• Score ≥ 20 — Very high chance of the news being real.
• Score between 11 and 19 — Uncertain or mixed credibility.
• Score ≤ 10 — Article is almost certainly fake or misleading.

The final score is calculated internally by combining article analysis, source credibility, and external validation. Total score is out of 30.
